Teaching

My teaching philosophy focuses on practical learning and active engagement, rooted in principles of design thinking, diversity, and integrity. I aim to foster an inclusive space where students can apply theory to real-world challenges. I am inspired by Chris McComb's meta-cognition and Mykel Kochenderfer's caring and student wellness approaches teaching philosophy. I am aspired to empower students in my classes.
