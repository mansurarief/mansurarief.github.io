I am a postdoc at Stanford Intelligent Systems Lab (SISL) working with Prof. Mykel Kochenderfer. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Mechanical Engineering Department at Carnegie Mellon University in 2023, working with Prof. Ding Zhao. in the CMU Safe AI Lab. I received my bachelor's degree from the Industrial and Systems Engineering Department at ITS and master's degree from the Industrial and Operations Engineering Department at the University of Michigan.

Much of my work combines machine learning and rare-event theories to efficiently simulate rare catastrophic events. The applications of this line of work include the accelerated safety testing of autonomous vehicles. Currently, I am working on a few AI for safety and sustainability projects, which mostly combine these efficient simulation framework with planning and decision-making algorithms. Here is the link to my CV. Feel free to reach out to me if you want to collaborate or have any questions.
